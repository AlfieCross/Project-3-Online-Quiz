How to Play

Ready to get started?

Here's how to play the quiz game:

  • You’ll be asked 5 fun questions, one at a time.
  • Choose one answer per question.
  • Click Next to move on.
  • On the final question, click Submit.
  • You’ll get a breakdown of your answers at the end!
